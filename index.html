The Premier Difference

IMAGE: Personal Photo

ViscoCity Fluid LLC was born out of a simple frustration: Why does a 20-minute oil change take up 2-hours at a dealership?


My passion for automotive work didn't start in a high-end shop—it started in a warehouse. After years in a fast-paced industrial environment, I found my fulfillment by working with my hands and keeping busy with tasks that required precision and care.


I know what it’s like to go in for a simple service and walk out two hours later after being hit with a list of "necessary" upsells. I knew there had to be a more practical, honest way to keep cars on the road.


My Specialty

Because I am a dedicated owner-operator, your vehicle is never just another number in a bay. You get the peace of mind knowing the person you booked is the person doing the work. I take my time to ensure a mess-free, professional result while you enjoy the comfort of your home. No waiting rooms, no drive time, and absolutely no upsells.
